Our Mission

A Capstone Project Built for Every Filipino Exam Taker

ReviewHub is a capstone project developed at Davao Central College in response to a real problem: Filipino working students and exam takers lack a structured, accessible, and affordable way to review consistently.

Aligned with SDG 4 — Quality Education, ReviewHub digitally adapts the proven methods of physical review centers — structured reinforcement, peer accountability, and active recall — and makes them available to every student, anywhere, on any device.

Common Questions

Everything You’re Wondering, Answered.

Is ReviewHub really free?

Yes. The core platform — micro-sessions, guided paths, ReviewBuddy matching, and study groups — is completely free. No credit card needed. A premium tier with offline access and advanced analytics is available, but everything you need to pass is free.

I only have 30 minutes a day. Will this actually work?

That’s more than enough. Sessions are designed for 10–15 minutes. Three short sessions a day adds up to 90 minutes of focused, structured review — far more effective than 3 hours of unfocused reading.

Which board exams does ReviewHub cover?

Currently: LET, NLE (Nursing), CPA, Engineering, and Architecture. More exams are being added based on community demand. Join the waitlist if yours isn’t listed yet.

What if I fall behind on my study plan?

ReviewHub automatically reschedules missed sessions and notifies your ReviewBuddy. The plan adapts to real life — it doesn’t pile up missed sessions and make you feel worse.

How is this different from YouTube or PDFs?

YouTube and PDFs don’t know what you’ve already studied, when your exam is, or that you skipped yesterday. ReviewHub combines structured paths, adaptive quizzes, a real accountability partner, and a community — none of which a PDF can provide.

Can I use ReviewHub offline?

Audio lessons and downloaded review modules work offline on mobile. A connection is needed to sync progress, access community features, and stream new content.
